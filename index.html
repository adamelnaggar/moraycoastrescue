About Moray Coast Rescue

Moray Coast Rescue is the only SLSGB Accredited Training Centre in Scotland, delivering surf rescue training on the Banffshire Coast. We are committed to providing opportunities for local people to develop surf rescue and water safety skills, taking advantage of the beautiful and rugged coastline Scotland has to offer, and addressing the gap in provision of coastal water safety education in Moray and Aberdeenshire.

Our team have a wealth of varied experience providing safety cover, running outdoor centres, and designing & delivering guided adventures in the coastal environment, including wild swimming, snorkelling, sea kayaking, coasteering, surfing, and paddleboarding.

With an in-depth knowledge of the Moray, Banffshire, and Aberdeenshire coastlines and a variety of specialist capabilities, expertise and qualifications, we can assist you with all of your water safety, technical advice, and training requirements.
